About Me

What made you want to pursue a career in Data Science?

I found it to be most interesting while studying Data Analytics as I love storytelling and problem solving with data while answering complex business problems. I felt like it brought the most value and was the most useful as someone who was in the midst of changing careers from a professional-athlete to becoming a Data Scientist in the future.

What is it about Data Analytics that made you fall in love with programming?

I have a passion for technology. I consider myself creative and analytical, so data analytics and programming have been something I always wanted to do. I also think it is a valuable skill to have and incredibly useful and applicable in many of today's industries.
